Life so far in Muthoot

College Chronicles: The Chaos, The Fun, The Glitter

Who knew that stepping into college would feel like diving headfirst into a rollercoaster of nerves, dance rehearsals, face paint, and (very stuck) water filters? From the very first day, it's been a wild blend of awkward introductions and unforgettable memories. So here’s my little diary of everything that’s happened so far. Buckle up — it’s been a ride!

1.That First Thursday: The Icebreaker Saga

The very first event of college landed on a Thursday — and I was absolutely nervous. New faces, new place, new everything. But surprisingly, it turned out to be a really great experience. We got to know each other, shared awkward laughs, and somehow by the end, we weren’t strangers anymore.

2.The Talent Show That Had It All

Shortly after, we had a talent show. And let me tell you — people came with their A-game. There was dancing, singing, art displays... and every single performance had that little spark. It was inspiring and wholesome and made you think, “Damn, these people are cool.”

3.Freshers’ Day Madness

Ah, Freshers’ Day. AKA the day we all collectively gave up sleep to learn choreography. Every first-year class had to perform, and our group went ALL OUT. We danced to an Indian song, we threw in "Shawty had them apple bottom jeans," and even had a co-ed group dance. We stayed back late every night to practice — it was exhausting, chaotic, and absolutely worth it. Our performance? Fire. The other performances? Also fire. The effort? Immeasurable. The memories? Forever.

4.Shreshta & Varnam: A Cultural Explosion

Then came Shreshta and Varnam, our college’s culture bombs. Dance, music, speeches, fashion shows — you name it, it was there. The fashion team was on fire, the performances were brilliant, and the vibe? Immaculate. There were food stalls, face painting, and makeup booths. My friends and I got these butterfly face paints — they were funky, a little chaotic (they kept sticking to my eyes), but SO worth it for the pics.

5.Concert Fever (x3)

We had not one, not two, but THREE concerts. And somehow — miracle of miracles — my friends and I managed to snag front-row spots for all of them. The energy, the volume, the lights, the view — it was chef’s kiss. We saw everything, we felt everything, we lived for those moments.

6.Water Conservation Day: Science, but Make It Messy

This one was… memorable. We were given stones, sand, charcoal, cotton, and a bottle, and the goal was to filter water. Sounds easy? It wasn’t. We may have overdone the sand layer (oops), and when our poor sir tried pouring the water in, it just... stayed there. No movement. No hope. He kept trying and eventually just squished the bottle in frustration until something came out. Sadly, it bypassed the sand completely, so our "filter" didn’t really filter. We didn’t win, but hey — it was hilarious and educational.

Looking Back, Smiling Forward

And now, just like that, exams are around the corner and the semester’s wrapping up. It feels like we just blinked and suddenly we’re here. Through every awkward intro, every chaotic dance practice, every glitter-filled event — we’ve grown, bonded, and made a million memories. I’m tired, grateful, and SO excited for what the next semesters are going to bring.

Because if the start was this fun, imagine what’s next.
